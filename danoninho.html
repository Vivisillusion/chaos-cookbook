5. Fake Danoninho

This sweet treat is a childhood classic in disguise. It's creamy, pink, and dangerously easy to make. Perfect for when you need dessert in five minutes and chaos in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of table cream (approx. 200g each)
  • 1 box (395g) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 packet of strawberry powdered juice (like Tang or Clight)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the table cream, condensed milk, and powdered juice mix.
  2. Blend until completely smooth and pink as sin.
  3. Pour into individual cups or a single dish. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until firm.
  4. Serve chilled. Add a sliced strawberry on top if you want to pretend it's healthy.
